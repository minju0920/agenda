MAGAZINE

AGENDA MONTHLY MAGAZINE
PUBLISHED IN NOVEMBER

WITH PHOTOGRAPHER Nghia

This November 2023 Agenda magazine was conducted with photographer Nghia. Nghia is working to embody happiness that exists individually for each person by visualizing the feeling of "the process of finding happiness" and sometimes "happiness" itself with a subjective gaze, and further encourages "action" to be happy.
The meaning or standard of 'happiness' varies from person to person. It can be a favorite place for some, or it can be the existence of a loving family or friend for others. As such, 'happiness' has something in common that it has a positive meaning for everyone, but each has a different meaning of existence. For this purpose, this month's magazine worked with various characters and landscapes under the theme of "happiness."
